🧠 About Minds

Nowadays, there are two kinds of minds: biological and machine.

  • Biological minds:  such as brains of humans and animals.
  • Machine minds:  such as modern AI systems — called Transformers (like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok).

The main job of every mind — whether biological or machine — is to learn and think.

And here’s something surprising: at their core, learning and thinking are actually just doing math. Yes, really!

And it’s simple math, not the scary kind from school.

The cool part? Machine minds aren’t just built — they learn on their own, so it’s more like we are training or growing them rather than building them like a robot.

How is this possible? In this course, you’ll discover the answer.

The Tiny helpers Of Every Mind

Every mind is, in essence, a NEURAL NETWORK.

It’s called "NEURAL" because it consists of NEURONS.

😎
NEURON

And it’s called "NETWORK" because the NEURONS connect to each other and work together.

We can think of each NEURON as a tiny yet cool creation.

NEURONS are like tiny helpers that learn and think on their own, helping the whole mind get smarter.

But what does every NEURON do?

Actually, every NEURON takes in input SIGNALS and then sends out one output SIGNAL.

Every SIGNAL is something that can be represented as a number.

In more detail, every NEURON:

  • Takes in input SIGNALS (say, 0.36 and 0.53)
  • Does simple math on them to calculate its output SIGNAL (say, 0.79)
  • Then sends out this output SIGNAL

For brevity:

  • The input SIGNALS are called INPUTS.
  • The output SIGNAL is called OUTPUT.

The Key Insight

An OUTPUT from one NEURON can become an INPUT for another NEURON that comes next — connecting the whole team together!

The Math Every Neuron Does

When a mind thinks, every NEURON does very simple math.

At its core, every NEURON works just like a store receipt.

TOTAL = 4.00 * 2.00 + 2.00 * 3.00 + (-0.50)
TOTAL = 8.00 + 6.00 - 0.50
TOTAL = 14.00 - 0.50
TOTAL = 13.50

At its core, every NEURON is also just a formula:

OUTPUT = INPUT_1 * WEIGHT_1 + INPUT_2 * WEIGHT_2 + BIAS

The mapping:

RECEIPT→NEURON
UNITS→INPUTS
PRICES→WEIGHTS
AMOUNTS→PRODUCTS
SUM→SUM
DISCOUNT→BIAS
TOTAL→OUTPUT

The WEIGHTS and BIAS are called PARAMETERS.

🌺 Our Very First Machine Mind

To quickly understand the essence of how machines learn and think, let’s start by growing the simplest possible mind: a one-neuron mind. A single NEURON. That’s it.

Moreover, this NEURON will only have a single PARAMETER: a single WEIGHT — no other WEIGHTS and no BIAS. A single WEIGHT. That’s it.
